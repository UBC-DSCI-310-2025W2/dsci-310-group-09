# Code of Conduct

## 1. Our Commitment

We are committed to fostering a welcoming, inclusive, respectful, and harrassment-free environment for everyone, regardless of age, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, race, ethnicity, nationality, religion, or professional background. 

Our goal is to create a safe, positive, and productive community that encourages open collaboration, knowledge sharing, and respectful discussion in all project spaces.

We value diversity and inclusivity and welcome different perspectives as it strengthens our work in data science and improves the quality and impact of our work.

## 2. Expected Behaviour

All participants in this project are expected to:

- Be respectful, considerate, and professional in all interactions.
- Communicate openly.
- Give and accept feedback gracefully.
- Respect differing viewpoints, experiences, and expertise.
- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Follow reproducible and ethical research practices.
- Protect sensitive and confidential information.

## 3. Unacceptable Behaviour

The following behaviours are not tolerated:

- Harassment, discrimination, or exclusion of any individual or group.
- Offensive or inappropriate comments.
- Personal attacks, insults, or trolling.
- Threats or intimidation.
- Publishing private or confidential information without consent.
- Deliberate sabotage or misue of project resources.
- Any behaviour that could harm the psychological safety or participation of others.

## 4. Consequences of Unacceptable Behaviour

Project maintainers are responsible for enforcing this Code of Conduct. Consequences for violations may include:

- A private warning or request to change behaviour.
- Temporary suspension from project spaces.
- Removal of content or contributions.
- Permanent ban from the project.
- Reporting serious cases to appropriate institutions or authorities.

_The severity of consequences will depend on the nature and impact of the behaviour._

## 5. Reporting and Dealing with Unacceptable Behaviour

If you experience or witness unacceptable behaviour, please report it as soon as possible. Reports will be handled confidentially and respectfully.

To report a concern:

- Contact the project maintainers via private message.
- Provide a clear description of the incident.
- Include any relevant documentation or evidence, if available.

Project Maintainers will:

1. Acknowledge receipt of the report.
2. Review the situation objectively and fairly
3. Take appropriate action.
4. Communicate outcomes where possible while respecting privacy.

We are committed to protecting individuals who report concerns from retaliation

## 6. Questions

If you have any questions about this Code of Conduct, please contact the project maintainers.
